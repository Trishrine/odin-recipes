Apple Arugala Salad

A sweet and spicy salad?

It's fall, and that means it's time to crack out the apples the cinnamon and the festive cheer. If you are looking for something savory but still packs a fresh punch this season, do we have the salad for you. Apples and a lovely balsamic vinaigerette will bring together all of the fall vibes you could possibly want.

Vinaigerette

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients together and thouroughly whisk them until well combined.
  2. Let rest until you are ready to incorporate it into the salad.

The Salad

Ingredients

  • A generous amount of arugala
  • 2 apples of your choice
  • Blue cheese crumbles
  • 1/2 lemon
  • vinaigerette
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Chop apples into small chunky pieces about an 1" wide.
  2. slice lemon in half and juice into bowl
  3. Combine arugala, apples, and blue cheese crumbles into bowl, mix thouroughly
  4. Just before serving add vinaigerette and combine well
  5. Once the vinaigerette is added the arugala will start to wilt, so if you are planning on saving any, make sure to keep some of the salad separate without adding any vinaigerette to it yet.
