Easy Eggs and Rice

Breakfast can be hard, especially when you want to eat something that's not cereal and make it out the door in time for work. Rice and eggs are a super happy alternative to this. One of the best things about them is that you can make a big batch of rice for the week, refrigerate it, and then warm it backup and just make eggs. They are incredibly easy to season with whatever, and that goes for the rice too! This recipe is amazing becuase it is so customizable, and you will feel so much better walking out the door after making something like this instead of cereal!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 basmati rice
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tablespoon of Butter
  • 3 eggs
  • (optional) salsa, hot sauce, spices, herbs, or veggies

Instructions

  1. Add rice, water, and salt and pepper to a small pot.
  2. Bring the water inside to a boil, and once boiling cover pot and set temperature to low, let simmer for 12-15 minutes or until little to no water is left in pot.
  3. When rice is almost done, add butter to a frying pan, and get frying pan hot enough to melt butter
  4. Crack each egg into the frying pan, cook on one side for about 1-2 minutes, before flipping and cooking the other side for about 30 seconds
  5. Remove eggs from heat
  6. Combine eggs and rice into one bowl and add any additional toppings you might like to have with it.
